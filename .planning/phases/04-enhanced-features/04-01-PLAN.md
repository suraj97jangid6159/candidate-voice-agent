**Files:** `static/index.html`, `static/app.js`, `main.py`, `db.py`

**Action:**

- Add `GET /api/candidates/{candidate_id}` returning full candidate with nested jobs and latest call status
- Modify `static/index.html` — add candidate list panel above the profile card with a selectable list
- Modify `static/app.js` — on candidate select, load their profile, jobs, and call history; disable jobCard until candidate is selected
- New candidate creation flow: form at top of list or redirect to existing profile form

**Acceptance:**
- `GET /api/candidates` returns all candidates with job counts
- Dashboard candidate list shows all saved candidates
- Selecting a candidate loads their profile data and call history
- Creating a new candidate adds them to the list
